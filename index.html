05  ·  Privacy as a feature

Your bank credentials never touch our servers.

We don't say "privacy-first" as a slogan. We architected the app so we couldn't betray it if we wanted to.

Plaid handles every bank connection, your login goes directly to your institution, not to us. We never see your password. We never store it. We can't, by design.

The tokens that do reach us are AES-256 encrypted at rest. Analytics is opt-in on first launch. No selling data, ever. Full stop, no exceptions, no fine print.
